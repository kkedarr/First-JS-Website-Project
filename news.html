Missed Opportunity: John Wick's Potential Appearance in Mortal Kombat 1 Falls Through

"NetherRealm Studios' attempt to include John Wick in Mortal Kombat 1 fell through, despite initial interest. Ed Boon, a key figure at NetherRealm, believed John Wick would be a great addition to the Mortal Kombat universe. However, Keanu Reeves, the actor behind John Wick, and Lionsgate, which owns the character, had other plans. Licensing rights and fees likely played a role in this decision. Nevertheless, John Wick made a cameo in Fortnite in 2019. Boon also toyed with the idea of having established actors portray characters in Mortal Kombat 1, even considering Keanu Reeves for the role of Kenshi. Ultimately, this remained a "pipe dream" due to the complexities of video game acting."

Hideki Kamiya, Director Behind Bayonetta, Parts Ways with PlatinumGames to Embark on New Projects

Hideki Kamiya, the co-founder of the renowned PlatinumGames and the creative genius behind the iconic Bayonetta franchise, bids a heartfelt farewell to the company to embark on exciting new ventures within the dynamic gaming industry. This monumental decision, officially announced on the social media platform Xitter, signifies the culmination of his remarkable journey with PlatinumGames, where he wielded his visionary prowess from its very inception. Kamiya's highly-anticipated departure is slated for the significant date of October 12, 2023. In a collective expression of enthusiasm, both PlatinumGames and the esteemed Hideki Kamiya himself share an unwavering optimism regarding the indelible mark he will continue to leave on the ever-evolving gaming landscape.

Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl 2 Unveils Exciting Garfield Moves in Latest Trailer

Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl 2 has unveiled an exciting spotlight trailer, this time featuring Garfield's updated moveset. The game is set to release on November 14th, 2023, across platforms including PC, Nintendo Switch, Xbox, and PlayStation. The clever Monday release adds to the buzz surrounding the game's upcoming launch. With a slew of new content and revamped movesets, Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl 2 aims to surpass its predecessor, provided it operates smoothly. Garfield's gameplay centers around his 'Sugar Rush' meter, which turns food pickups into bursts of incredible speed. While Garfield may seem a bit overpowered initially, players can trigger a shorter version of the meter for accessibility. However, balancing his speed with strategic pie-throwing is crucial, making for an intriguing gameplay experience.
